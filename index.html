About Return to the Moon

Learn More About NASA's Plan To Put The First Woman On The Moon

With the Artemis program, Nasa will land the first woman on the Moon by 2024, using innovative technologies to explore more of the lunar suraface than ever before. This program is a collaboration between NASA, commercial, and international partners and will establish sustainable exploartion by the end of the decade. Then, NASA will use what they learn on and around the Moon to take the giant leap - sending astronauts to Mars.
